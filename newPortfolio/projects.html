What have I done?

SI 487 User Experience Capstone Project

For senior capstone course in the User Experience path, my group partnered with the Princeton University Library Finding Aids website. We worked to identify the pain points that users encounter as they navigate through the website through usability tests and interviews, then used this information to make informed design choices and improve the overall user experience.

Quicken Loans Summer Internship

During the summer of 2017, I worked at Quicken Loans in Detroit, Michigan as a User Experience Research Intern. My group ran the Echo Project, an internal communication initiative. I also participated in a Hackathon to design/prototype an intern mobile app.

Digital Product Design Independent Project
