Who am I?

Good question! I'm a proud East-coaster, originally from the historical little town of Lexington, Massachusetts. However, I decided to venture out into the *mysterious* Midwest for college, and although it's been rough at times, I'm glad I did.

After four years, I am now a senior at the University of Michigan. I am in the School of Information, completing a degree with a focus on User Experience. Last summer, I had the awesome opportunity of working as a User Experience Research intern at Quicken Loans in downtown Detroit.

When I graduate, I hope to be working in the greater Detroit area and travelling whenever possible!
